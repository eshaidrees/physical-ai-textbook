# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics** textbook. This comprehensive resource covers the fundamentals of physical artificial intelligence and humanoid robotics, from basic concepts to advanced implementations.

## About This Textbook

This textbook is designed to provide a modern, AI-native learning experience for students and professionals interested in:

- Physical AI principles and applications
- Humanoid robot design and control
- Robot Operating System (ROS 2) fundamentals
- Digital twin simulation techniques
- Vision-language-action systems
- Complete AI-robot pipeline integration

## Course Structure

The textbook is organized into six comprehensive chapters:

1. [Introduction to Physical AI](./intro-to-physical-ai/index.md) - Explore the fundamentals of Physical AI and its applications in robotics
2. [Basics of Humanoid Robotics](./robotics/anatomy.md) - Learn about the anatomy, kinematics, and control systems of humanoid robots
3. [ROS 2 Fundamentals](./ros-fundamentals/architecture.md) - Understand the fundamentals of Robot Operating System 2 (ROS 2)
4. [Digital Twin Simulation](./digital-twin-simulation/environment-overview.md) - Explore digital twin simulation using Gazebo and Isaac Sim environments
5. [Vision-Language-Action Systems](./vision-language-action/multimodal-ai.md) - Discover multimodal AI systems that integrate vision, language, and action
6. [Capstone: Simple AI-Robot Pipeline](./capstone/integration.md) - Integrate all concepts learned into a complete AI-robot pipeline
