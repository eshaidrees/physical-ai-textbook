# AI-Powered Chatbot

Welcome to the AI-powered chatbot for the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics textbook. This RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) system allows you to ask questions about any topic covered in the textbook.

## How to Use

1. Type your question in the chat interface below
2. The system will search the textbook content for relevant information
3. An AI-powered response will be generated based on the textbook content

## Capabilities

The chatbot can help you with:
- Explaining complex concepts from the textbook
- Finding specific information across all chapters
- Providing examples and applications of theoretical concepts
- Clarifying technical terminology

## Limitations

- The chatbot only has access to information in this textbook
- Responses are generated based on the content of the six chapters
- For the most accurate information, always refer to the original textbook content

## Getting Started

Try asking questions like:
- "Explain the concept of Physical AI"
- "How does inverse kinematics work in humanoid robots?"
- "What are the key components of ROS 2 architecture?"
- "Describe the differences between Gazebo and Isaac Sim"
- "How do vision-language-action systems work?"
- "What are the components of the AI-robot pipeline?"

Start your exploration by using the chat interface below!
